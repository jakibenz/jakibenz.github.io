Our Story

Kenji and Tricia's journey began in 2017 when they met at their workplace. What started as casual lunch breaks soon blossomed into something special, with laughter becoming their signature connection. Their shared love for adventure led them to Japan, which unknowingly became a significant backdrop to their love story.

When Tricia accepted a job in Singapore, they navigated a year of long-distance relationship before Kenji joined her abroad. Together, they weathered the pandemic, confirming what they already knew—they were meant to face life's challenges side by side. After six beautiful years, their story came full circle when Kenji proposed in Kyoto, Japan—the country where their travels first began.

"The best love stories aren't the ones that never face challenges, but the ones where two people decide that what they have is worth crossing oceans for."
